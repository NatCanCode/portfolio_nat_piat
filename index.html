My name is Nat!

  • I'm a junior Web Developer living in Paris, and I used to work in international communications.
  • At the crossroad between UX/UI design and Front-End Development, I aim to create neat, minimalist and responsive websites. I strive for simplicity, clarity, and purity in my work.
  • The technologies I usually use are HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python, Sass, Vue.js, React and Node.js, and I prototype with Figma.
  • I’m a coffee AND tea addict, inspired by my peers and colleagues, my little one, my partner, my grumpy cat, anything and everything photography related, and MedTech activism.
  • I take pride in building and developing projects that can help make the world a better place.
